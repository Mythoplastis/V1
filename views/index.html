Terms and Conditions

You can submit your material in any format you want; being photo, video, text and sound.

You have the option to allow others to download your submitted material, modify it and create a new material. In creating your input also you can ask others for visual helps and collaborate with them in creating the material.

The materials you submit will be visible to public as long as the website is running. You can delete your entries at any time but if you have allowed others to download your material and modify it, their entry will not be affected by you deleting your entry.

All the submitted materials will be reviewed by website administrators and admins have all the right to remove any material which they find offensive in any terms or inappropriate.

You can also report any abuse of your submitted material.
